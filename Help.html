Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a specific room?
Navigate to the "Guide Map" page. You can select the floor you wish to view, then click on room labels to see more details. A search function is coming soon!
Is the map available for all buildings?
Currently, the interactive map covers C and D Buildings. Additional buildings will be added in future updates.
Can I report an issue with the map?
Yes! Please go to the "Feedback" page and submit a report. Your input helps us improve.
How do I change my profile information?
Visit the "Account" page and click "Edit Profile" to update your personal information.
